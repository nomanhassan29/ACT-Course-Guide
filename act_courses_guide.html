Frequently asked questions
🔑

Forgot your Protect UK password?

▶
  1. Go to the Protect UK login page
  2. Click "Request new password" on the right side of the login button
  3. Enter your email address and click Submit
  4. Check your inbox — you will receive an email with a link to reset your password
  5. Click the link and set a new password. Make sure you save it this time!

💡 Check your spam or junk folder if you don't see the email within a few minutes.

🔒

Account blocked on Protect UK?

▶

Your account gets blocked when the wrong password is entered too many times. There are two ways to sort this:

Option 1 — Contact Protect UK
  1. Use the link below to submit a support request to Protect UK
  2. Fill in your name, email address and explain that your account is blocked
  3. Click Submit
  4. They will get back to you within 24–48 hours with a link to reset your password
↗ Submit a Support Request
Option 2 — Create a New Account

⚠ If you do not receive a reset link or any confirmation from Protect UK within 48 hours, the only option is to create a new account using a different email address and a different phone number.

📄

Not seeing the download button after completing the course?

▶

First try the standard steps:

  1. Click the menu icon at the top right of Protect UK
  2. Select "User Profile" from the dropdown
  3. Click "Certificates Received"
  4. Look for the Download button on the right side of each course name
  5. Download both and transfer to your phone
Still not seeing it?

⚠ You need to refresh each course. Follow the steps below for both courses separately.

ACT Awareness: Click Here ↗

ACT Security: Click Here ↗

How to refresh the course:
  1. Open the course using the link above and click "Continue Training"
  2. On each module click Exit, then click Next to move forward
  3. Keep doing this until you reach the last module
  4. On the last module click Next — you will see a list of all modules
  5. At the bottom click "Go to Training Homepage"
  6. Repeat the same steps for the second course

💡 Once both are refreshed, go to User Profile → Certificates Received and the download buttons will now appear.
